How close are NYC subway entrances to stations?

Supposedly New York City has one of the most complex and difficult to navigate subway systems, where walking into a wrong station entrance is a common occurence, with the right one being rather far away. I wondered how accurate this statement is and thus set out to visualise the subway stations and their entrances as a personal project. Throughout making the visualisation I learned Mapbox GL JS.
